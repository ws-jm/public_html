Complex passwords such as ‘BaKgc44VK4#1’ can be difficult to remember so it is now considered good practice to use 'passphrases' as they are much harder to crack but are easier to remember.

To create a passphrase, use a unique but memorable phrase that is at least 24 characters long. You'll need to add a random element so include some uppercase and lowercase characters, numbers and symbols.
